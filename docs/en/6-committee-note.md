# 6 · Note for the assembly committee

Dear colleagues,

We bring you an idea, with humility and no pressure at all.

After the June 24 earthquakes, we wanted to see if we could turn what the network built at its first meeting into something useful to the country, now. The idea is simple: group our people by expertise so they start working right away, on five concrete fronts, from now and with horizons of two to three months, one year and two years, always channeling everything through already-established bodies.

Two things we want to make very clear.

The first is the philosophy: low profile. This is not about making the network or anyone known. Credit belongs to whoever does the work. We work quietly, and that also protects those inside the country.

The second is that this is only a proposal. If the committee feels the network is not the right vehicle for this, or that it is not the moment, we understand completely and it is fine. In that case the effort will continue modestly and independently, inviting the Red through Creative Commons Venezuela (CC Venezuela).

We ask only for a read and your view: yes, no, or how you would adjust it. The full plan, with the groups, the challenge and all the steps, is in this same repository, in Spanish and English.

Thank you,
The Conexión RedJIV coordination
