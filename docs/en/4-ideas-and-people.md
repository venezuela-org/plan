## The network's talent

48 unique people (duplicate registrations consolidated, plus idea authors who joined as guests). Double spread: inside the country and diaspora.

Strengths by area: chemistry and materials 14, physics and complex systems 10, health and biomedicine 9, biology and ecology 5, computing and data 3, geosciences 3, mathematics 2, plus control engineering and paleontology. Half inside the country gives ground access; the diaspora contributes instruments, affiliations and reach, spread across some 14 countries (Spain, Switzerland, Austria, Brazil, USA, Netherlands, Germany, Belgium, Italy, Chile, France, Peru, Argentina).

The full per-person inventory, with area and approximate country, is in the file `Inventario_talento_RedJIV.xlsx` that accompanies this repo. Country and affiliation were inferred from email domains and phone codes, so treat them as a starting point to confirm.
